    # Safety

    # WARNING

    To maintain a reasonable level of safety the following should be noted:

    • This unit runs on electricity. Of necessity some components within the device have dangerous voltages applied to them.
    • Non observance of the operating instructions can cause death, serious injuries and/or extensive damage to property!
    • Never open the housing or one of the under heaters when the mains plug is connected.
    • Do not use the units for any purpose other than that for which they are intended. Use it according to the operating instructions.
    • This equipment must not be switched on or used without supervision.
    • When the hot air pen is in use high temperatures are reached within. Therefore, do not touch the heating element; there is the danger of being burnt. Do not direct any hot air unto any part of the body.
    • Do not place any flammable material in the vicinity of the under heater or the hot air/gas nozzle.
    • Please use the under heater and hot air pen only on a level and non flammable surface.
    • Touch the just deposited nozzles or soldering tools first after they have cooled down. Place the soldering pen, the nozzles and soldering tools only unto the support provided specifically for the purpose.
    • When the under heater is in use its housing, the exit of the cooling air and the areas exposed to the infrared rays can reach high temperatures. Please do not touch the housing until it has cooled - burn hazard.
    • Avoid skin contact with adhesives or solder pastes. Contact with the skin can lead to damaged health.
    • Any maintenance of the units must be carried out only by suitably qualified personnel or you must request the attendance of a technician from Martin GmbH.
    • Please also pay attention to the safety regulations applicable in your area.

    # Technology

    The supply unit for dispensing, vacuum and soldering pens as well as for under heating is the DBL 06 which forms the basis if Line Rework pc. It is a precise, practicable and portable combination unit for proto-typing, repair, service, small scale production and especially for rework in branch of quantity production. It fulfils all your requirements for dealing with SMDs:

    • Desoldering of SMDs
    • Removal of used solder
    • Application of solder paste or adhesive
    • Picking and placing
    • Soldering of SMDs
    • Reballing of BGAs, CSPs and QFNs. The SMD Compact Station DBL 06 enables you to deal with almost with all SMD types from 0201, SOs, PLCCs, QFPs, BGAs, CSPs, QFNs, μSMDs to plugs and non standard components. You only need the required pick & place and solder tools. Even Reballing of BGA and CSP can be realized with the required mini oven. Your DBL 06, as component of our Line Rework pc, can be augmented with the optional extras in a modular manner to build up high value and comprehensive MARTIN rework station.

    Exact energy delivery to the soldering point is by means of a hot pen, which uses compressed air or inert gas such as nitrogen. Particularly useful is the ability to quickly change the nozzles of the hot air pen as well as easily being able to clamp it to the magnetic keep of the positioning arm.

    The under heater provides support for hot gas soldering. Your DBL 06 will accept the small IRH under heater (for e.g. Mobile Phones) or one of the area under heaters HIF. This involves the heating of the substrate from below to an uncritical temperature (130 - 190°C). This reduces the likelihood of temperature overshoots at the SMD from the top of the substrate.

    Operating Instructions Rework PC Hardware December/ 2015

    During its development and that of the soldering tools particular care has been taken to take into account the needs of PCBs and components. This has been achieved by precise temperature measurement and regulation and the least possible amount of heat being used and applied by the heating element and soldering tools. Solder joints with excellent reliability are achieved by using the program soldering function (replicating production reflow soldering processes). Precise amounts of energy are carefully delivered by means of hot air directly from the top to the solder joints and from below by infrared radiation to the circuit board.

    The DBL 06 provided you with an intelligent and very precise Dis Module. The ‘clever’ part means that using the equipment could not be simpler. The extensive computations and interrelationships the dispenser takes account of are of concern to the user only insofar as your dispensing device does what you want it to do – precision dispensing independent of:

    • Temperature of the medium to be dispensed (10 - 40 °C)
    • Pressure of available compressed air (4 - 8 bar).

    1. Description of Function

    # Vac Module

    The Combination Suction Pen has two functions:

    • place SMDs (together with a suction nozzle)
    • remove unwanted solder (together with a solder removal nozzle).

    The nozzles can quickly be changed, by using just one hand and the Nozzle Park.

    Operating Instructions Rework PC Hardware December/ 2015

    # Dis Module

    Solder pastes, flux creme, adhesives or other media can be applied in precisely controlled and consistent quantities with the dispensing pen. There is an effective thermal break between the grip of the dispensing pen and the medium in the syringe.

    1. Description of Function

    # Auto Vision Placer

    For frequent and comfortable use especially with Fine Pitch components we recommend the use of our Auto Vision Placer. A high resolution camera together with 5 automatical axes enables absolutely sure placements of SMDs with the minimum of effort as well as automatic lifting of the components after desoldering.

    With the concept of a PC controlled Auto Vision Placer being realised, MARTIN have managed to tidy up a critical step in QFP, BGA, CSP, QFN and μSMD rework. Rework can now be carried out quickly and safely, for the first time independent from the naked eye, with the automatic Auto Vision Placer.

    Operating Instructions Rework PC Hardware December/ 2015

    # Auto Star Placer

    The Auto-Star-Placer operates with five motorised axes just as the Auto-Vision-Placer. It offers effortless processing and secure placement of SMDs, as well as automatic lifting of components after desoldering.

    Placement is directed by using the Star Tools and the naked eye.

    With the concept of a PC controlled Auto Vision Placer being realised, MARTIN have managed to tidy up a critical step in QFP, BGA, CSP, QFN and μSMD rework. Rework can now be carried out quickly and safely, for the first time independent from the naked eye, with the automatic Auto Vision Placer.

    1. Description of Function

    # Smart Fix Compact 05 / 08 / 09

    For the precision handling of SMD packages Smart Fix Compact is an important accessory. PCBs are supported securely along the whole of one edge and by magnetic clamps in almost arbitrary form.

    The Positioning Arm (AVP or ASP) is guided by a straight edge, roughly aligned on the Smart Fix and magnetically locked. Fine positioning is carried out with the motorised 5 axes.

    Fans help the PCBs to cool rapidly after soldering.

    Operating Instructions Rework PC Hardware December/ 2015

    # Under heater – IRH

    Heating a large area of the under side of a printed circuit board takes the Martin Rework Process closer to the conditions prevailing in large reflow ovens. Thus allows temperatures of the above board heating arrangements to be lowered and PCB distortions are avoided.

    After the soldering process the under heater is cooled by compressed air.

    # 3. Description of Function

    # Under heater – HIF 08 / 09

    Heating a large area of the button side of a printed circuit board takes the Martin Rework Process closer to the conditions prevailing in large reflow ovens. This allows temperatures of the above board heating arrangements to be lowered and PCB distortions are avoided.

    By moving the hand rest of Smart Fix Compact it can be made suitable for any size of board. The heaters thus not needed, will be switched off automatically.

    In operation the unit is monitoring the applied mains voltage. Any fluctuations will be compensated for to ensure constant soldering results.
